Our restaurant

Our restaurant is located in one of the most picturesque places in New York City. We're on the banks of the river, your oasis inside the city where you can escape the noisy and dusty metropolis. At our restaurant you can eat great food and order delicious food to take home. We are really unique because everything is thought out to the smallest detail: the project is built of wild Transcarpathian log cabin, fireplace in the main hall of the restaurant and panoramic windows overlooking the river, cozy gazebo on the river bank and the best viewing terrace, tent with seating for 200 people, fabulous children's cottage and swimming pool.
